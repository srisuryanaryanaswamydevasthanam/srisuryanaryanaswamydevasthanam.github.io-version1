Rules and Instructions

Appeal to devotees Sri Suryanarayana Swamy's Temple has been allowing the devotees under following rules and regulations.
Instructions to the Devotees at the Temple as part of the Corona Prevention :
  1. Devotees not allowed to do pradakshana in the Temple.
  2. Devotees should wear face mask and should bring aadhar card.
  3. Visitors/Devotees must wash their hands and feet with water in hand wash area before stepping inside the Temple premises.
  4. Visitors/Devotees must sanitize their hands with sanitizer which is provided infront of temple and go through thermal screening test.
  5. Prohibition of visitation of young children and the elderly in the temple.
  6. Only a darshan is provided in the temple for devotees.
  7. No theertham or satari will be provided, in order to avoid contact.
  8. Devotees should Maintain physical distancing of a minimum of six feet at all times when queuing up for entry.
